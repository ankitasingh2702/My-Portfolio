Basil Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 12-15 fresh basil leaves
  • 1½ cups whipping cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • ⅓ cup condensed milk
  • 2 tbsps powdered sugar
  • Fresh basil sprigs for garnish

Method

  1. Whip whipping cream in a mixing bowl till soft peaks are formed.
  2. Put basil leaves in a blender jar, add milk and blend to a coarse paste. Add this to the whipped cream. Add fresh cream, condensed milk and powdered sugar and mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture into an air tight container, cover it with a lid and place it in the freezer to freeze for 6-7 hours.
  4. Place scoops of the icecream in serving bowls and serve garnished with basil sprigs.

Almond smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Almonds , soaked overnight and drained
  • 2 medium Ripe bananas
  • 1 cup + to garnish Fresh blueberries
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla essence
  • 3 tablespoons Honey to garnish Fresh mint sprigs
  • 2 tablespoons Basil seeds (sabza) , soaked

Method

  1. Put soaked almonds in a grinder jar, add 2 cups warm water and grind till mixture is smooth.
  2. Line a strainer kept on a bowl with muslin cloth, pour the ground almond mixture into it and strain the almond milk into the bowl. Grind the residue again with 1 cup warm water to get more almond milk and strain this into the same bowl.
  3. Peel the bananas, break into half and put in the grinder jar. Add blueberries, vanilla essence, honey, almond milk and grind to a smooth mixture.
  4. Skew blueberries and a mint sprig on each skewer.
  5. Put some basil seeds in each serving glass and pour the smoothie over them. Place one skewer on each glass and serve immediately.

Instant Dosa

Ingredient

  • 1½ cups semolina
  • 2 tbsps whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • Ghee for cooking
  • Coconut chutney for serving

Method

  1. Put semolina, whole wheat flour, gram flour and sugar into a grinder jar and grind coarsely.
  2. Transfer into a bowl and add salt and buttermilk and mix till well blended. Set aside for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Heat a non-stick tawa. Pour a ladleful of the batter and spread it in a clockwise motion to make as thin a disc as possible.
  4. Drizzle ghee along the sides and cook for 2 minutes. Fold in half and place on a serving plate.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney

Kung Pao Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250 gm chopped into cubes paneer
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon corn flour
  • 2 dry red chili
  • 1 medium chopped capsicum (green pepper)
  • 1 handful chopped spring onions
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • gm salt
  • water as required
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 1 medium thinly sliced onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • gm black pepper

Method

  1. Step 1:- Coat the paneer with masala In a bowl, add paneer cubes along with chili powder, salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp cornflour. Mix everything well.
  2. Step 2:- Shallow fry paneer cubes Heat oil in a pan and then add paneer cubes. Shallow fry them till they turn golden brown in color. Once done, transfer them to an absorbent paper. Now, saute garlic on the same pan.
  3. Step 3:- Combine the rest ingredients Now, add onions and red chillies. Saute them until the onions turn translucent. Next, add capsicum. and saute it for 2 mins. Once done, add chili sauce, peanuts, soy sauce, and 1 tbsp cornflour (mix 1/2 tablespoon of cornflour in 1 tablespoon of water). Mix to combine everything well. Cook for 3 mins.
  4. Step 4:- Add the fried paneer cubes Finally, add fried paneer cubes along with salt. Mix everything well and cook for about 1-2 mins.
  5. Step 5:- Your Kung Pao Paneer is ready to serve Garnish it with spring onions. Your Kung Pao Paneer is ready. Enjoy.
  6. Tips:-Prefer salted peanuts over normal peanuts to add extra flavor to the recipe. You can also add in more veggies like corn, bell peppers, if you want.

Kesar Pista Kulfi

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 litre full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped pistachios
  • 2 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 10 strand saffron
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom

    • Method

    1. Step 1:- Boil milk In a pan, add full cream milk and let it come to a boil. Once done, let it simmer for about 25-30 mins. Stir occasionally. Meanwhile, soak saffron strands in 2 tablespoon warm milk.
    2. Step 2:- Prepare the kulfi mixture Now, add sugar to milk and mix it well. Let it simmer till the milk thickens and then add pistachios, cardamom powder, and soaked saffron milk. Mix everything well and let it simmer again for 5 mins. Switch off the gas flame and let the milk cool for a while.
    3. Step 3:- second Pour the mixture in moulds Pour the mixture into kulfi moulds once it has thickened well. Freeze it for at least 4-5 hours.
    4. Step 4:- Your Kesar Pista Kulfi is ready Once done, insert a wooden stick inside the kulfi mould and gently take the kulfi out. Top it with chopped pistachios and saffron strands. Your Kesar Pista Kulfi is ready. Enjoy.
    5. Tips i:- You can also pour the kulfi mixture into glasses and cover with a cling wrap.
    6. ii:- The thickened milk can even take an hour to cool. So, be patient and let it cool completely. You can also replace sugar with condensed milk.

Kiwi Granola Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kiwi
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup mixed dry fruits
  • 4 tablespoon honey
  • 2 handfuls roasted peanuts
  • 1 1/2 cup granola
  • 1 1/2 cup low fat cream

    • Method

    1. Step 1:- Whip the mix To begin with this easy recipe, wash and chop fresh kiwis. Keep them aside. Next, take a bowl and using a whisker, whisk the low fat cream with vanilla essence, honey. Make it foamy and set it aside.
    2. Step 2:- Take a chilled glass, place the granola as a the first layer, top it with nuts and dry fruits, then add a scoop of cream mixture along with kiwis. Then repeat the process.
    3. Step 3:- Garnish with kiwi Lastly, garnish with kiwi and sprinkle some honey. Refrigerate and enjoy!
    4. Tips:- If you want to make it more creamy, you can also add cream cheese. You can also add chia seeds.

Chicken Lajawab

Ingredients

  • 600 gm chicken
  • 2 tablespoon garlic
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoon yoghurt (curd)
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 onion
  • 2 green chilli
  • 4 tablespoon refined oil
  • 2 tablespoon ginger
  • 2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 3 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 3 tomato
  • 2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • salt as required

Method

  1. marinated chicken Step 1:-Clean and marinate the chicken Wash the chicken nicely and pat dry the meat. Add it to a bowl and marinate it by adding ginger garlic paste, 1/2 tsp cumin powder, yoghurt, ½ tsp turmeric powder, ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli, ½ tsp salt and 1 tbsp besan. Let this sit for 15 minutes and then cook these pieces in hot oil. Take them out of the pan and keep aside.
  2. Step 2:- Prepare the curry In 2 tbsp of oil, add some mustard seeds and let them splutter. Once they calm down, add the cumin seeds, hing, ginger-garlic paste and finely chopped onions. Cook this until the onions start to turn translucent. Add the spices at this point and quickly add chopped tomatoes. Cook this until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts coming to the surface.
  3. Step 3:- Add the chicken, simmer and serve! At this point, add the cooked chicken pieces, and coat them well in the mixture. Add 1 cup of water and cover. Let this cook for about 10 minutes or until the oil comes to the surface. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve with rice or chapati.

Keema Biryani

Ingredient

  • 500 gm basmati rice
  • 200 gm yoghurt (curd)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 2 green cardamom
  • 4 clove
  • 10 peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoon biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 handful coriander leaves
  • 1 kilograms minced lamb
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/4 cup refined oil
  • 2 star anise
  • 2 bay leaf
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 handful mint leaves
  • ml water

Method

  1. Step 1:-Boil water in a pan Heat oil in a pan with a thick bottom and simultaneously boil approximately 2 litres of water in a vessel.
  2. Step 2:- Saute onions with whole spices and then cook keema in it Add onions to the oil, saute till translucent, gradually add cumin seeds, peppercorns, green cardamom, cloves and ginger-garlic paste and 2 tsp of chilli powder. Saute for 2 minutes, add minced meat, curd and salt to taste. Continue to saute till all the water gets soaked up and keema is 3/4 portion down.
  3. Step 3:-Boil rice with whole spices Once the water starts to boil, add bay leaves, green cardamom, star anise, and salt to taste. Then add washed rice, when rice is 60 per cent done, drain the water using the sieve.
  4. Step 4 :-Spread the cooked keema on rice layer with fried onions, cook for 15-20 minutes and the Keema Biryani is ready Spread a layer of half of the rice in a big vessel. Put keema, some coriander and mint leaves, some masala and green chilli achar on top. Add the remaining rice to the top, add a little desi ghee and brown onions on top for the amazing fragrance, cover it with a lid and let it cook for 20 minutes on low flame. Serve with cucumber raita and enjoy.
